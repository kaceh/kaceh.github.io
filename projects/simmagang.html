ITS Internship Site

Managing university internships has never been this easy.

Seamless Digital PRocess

Made for Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember University, this site digitalizes the internship management process, a crucial aspect of graduation, and reduces stress for over 20,000 active students.

What I've Learned

Through this project, I learned how to communicate effectively with people across different divisions. I had to tailor my communication to each group, discussing project-related matters in their specific language. In other words, beyond understanding the user, I also had to empathize with the stakeholders' perspectives. Furthermore, as the project manager, I was responsible for communicating technical details to the backend team.

Deploying the site presented another challenge for my team and me. Since the site was intended to be hosted under the university's domain, we had to collaborate with the website administrators to ensure a successful launch. This highlighted the importance of clear communication.

My Roles

Project Manager, UI/UX Designer, and Frontend Developer. We employed Agile methodologies, and as the project manager, I planned our sprints. I also served as the primary point of contact for stakeholders and the project head. This closely related to my role as the UI/UX Designer, as I needed to address the needs of three distinct user groups through this application.

Understanding and providing solutions for the faculty, supervisors, and students required a thorough process. I conducted interviews, surveys, and analyzed the existing conventional workflow to effectively propose a new solution through a digitalized platform, which proved to reduce the overall user time on task by 40%.
